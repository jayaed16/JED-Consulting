About

I build websites and systems that make work feel lighter.

I’m Jaya — a software engineer and business analyst who loves helping small teams turn messy ideas into clear, functional websites and business systems.

Hi, I’m Jaya.

I started J.E.D. Consulting after seeing how often good businesses struggled with clunky websites and confusing systems. I’ve worked as both a software developer and a business analyst, which means I care about the code and the people who have to live with whatever we build.

My background is in Software Engineering and business, and I’ve spent a lot of time inside real organizations — mapping processes, gathering requirements, and then actually building the solutions.

Now, through J.E.D. Consulting, I focus on three things: clear websites, practical automations, and CRMs that your team doesn’t secretly hate.

My approach:

Human-centered systems. Tools should empower people — not get in their way.
Data-driven decisions. Good design starts with understanding how your business operates today.
Right-sized solutions. I design systems that match your stage, complexity, and internal capacity.

How I can help

Most projects fall into one of these buckets — and often we’ll mix pieces from more than one.

Web Design

Custom sites for small businesses that need to look professional, feel on-brand, and be easy to update without touching code.

Business Automation

Mapping your processes, then using tools like Power Platform to remove repetitive tasks and make work flow the way it should.

CRM in Microsoft CE

Building or refining CRMs in Microsoft CE and Power Platform so your team can track leads, customers, and work without fighting the system.

Want to work together?

If you have a website, system, or CRM project in mind, I’d love to hear about it — even if it’s still a rough idea.
